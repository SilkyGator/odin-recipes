Pizza

A quick and easy pizza solution; it's not digornio, it's tortilla

Tortilla pizza is a great solution for nights when the pizza delivery may take too long, or you're just looking for a cheaper and healthier option to take-out pizza. With only a 30-minute time to cook, you're well prepared to have a kid-friendly dinner ready in no time at all.

Made with gooey cheese, rich tomato sauce, and whole-grain tortillas, these pizzas are delicious as-is, but it is easy to tweak them to your liking by adding any toppings desired.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Oil
  • Seasonings (garlic powder, black pepper, oregano)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Toppings of choice
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Brush tortilla with olive oil, season, and bake.
  2. Top with tomato sauce and toppings, and sprinkle with cheese.
  3. Bake again until the cheese is melted, and season with oregano.
