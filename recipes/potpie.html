Chicken Pot Pie

A delicious dish with chicken and vegetables, encased in a soft crust

Chicken pot pie is a soup-like dish, featuring chicken, carrots, peas, onions, celery, and whatever else you may choose. The finished dish has a thick consistency, and is encased in a soft pie-like crust, providing the namesake.

While the recipe takes some time, it is hearty and filling, and keeps well for leftovers; additionally, as the ingredients are easy to find, a normal shopping trip can provide everything needed without breaking the bank.

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken breast (cut into cubes)
  • Peas
  • Carrots (chopped)
  • Celery (chopped)
  • Onions (chopped)
  • Seasonings
  • Butter and Flour
  • Broth and Milk
  • Pie crusts

Steps

  • Make the filling:

    1. Boil chicken with peas, carrots, and celery for 15 minutes
    2. Cook onions in butter until transparent
    3. Stir in flour and seasoning
    4. Add chicken broth and milk and simmer until filling is thick

  • Fill and Bake Pie:

    1. Place filling in pie crust
    2. Pour chicken broth mixture over filling
    3. Cover with top crust and seal edges
    4. Cut slits in top to allow steam to escape
    5. Bake until pastry is golden brown and filling is bubbly
