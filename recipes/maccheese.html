Macaroni and Cheese

A delicious, fast, and easy macaroni and cheese recipe

This macaroni and cheese is a fast but elegant take on a childhood classic. The recipe can be made quickly, and is sure to be enjoyed by children and adults alike.

Rich and creamy cheese, combined with soft noodles, brings this classic dish into a new level of respect for all ages.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. macaroni noodles
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Milk
  • Shredded cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Cook noodles for about 8 minutes.
  3. Make roux by melting butter, adding flour, salt, and pepper, and stirring in milk until mixture is bubbling.
  4. Add cheese to the roux.
  5. Drain noodles and stir into cheese sauce.
